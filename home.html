Rachel's Art

About Me

I am a 15 year old artist living in Walpole Massachusetts. I love drawing and painting and have been taking art classes since I was 9. I currently sell stickers and some prints on my Etsy shop. A lot of my art revolves around characters, shows, and books I enjoy, but I also love realism. My favorite subject to draw is definitely people. I love working in differnet paints and inks the most while I don't really like colored pencils. My style is constantly changing but I will never loose my intrest in art.
