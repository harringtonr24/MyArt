Inspiration

Drawing Wiff Waffles

Drawing Wiff Waffles has been a big inspiratin to me ever since I started watching youtube. Rin takes on obstacles and challenges to show how art can be whatever you want it too. She turns mistakes into something that makes the piece better and ends up with amazing illustrations no matter how big the challenge. Her youtube account currently has 2.55 million followers and over 370 million views in total.

Tom Bancroft

Tom Bancrot is a Disney Animation Veteran. He is the Creator of the dragon “Mushu” from Mulan and has helpd craft so many more disney character designs. Tom also created the MerMay challenge where you draw a mermaid for every day of may that a lot of artists participate in. He is not only a character designer, but also a Teacher

Audiy

Audrey is a character designer and has a big following on all her social media platforms. Like Rin, Audrey also takes on a lot of interesting challenges and character design prompts. Her Youtube account currently has 1.25 million subscribers and about 270 million views overall.

Padeliciouss

Pedro has a really smooth and aestheticly pleasing art style that really works with the characters they draw. Pedro draws a lot of characters that I find myself drawing as well, in interesting poses and styles. They are a digital artist which is what I am aiming to get better at.

Schmoedraws

Simone draws happy character illustrations with so much storytelling in every artwork. Along with the characters they draw amazing backgrounds tha really give the character a sense of space.

Loisvb

Loish is an amazing digital artist that captures feeling in her works. Her art style is flowy and blocky mashed together and it works really well. Loish uses colors extremly well to make some things faded other things stand out. She currently has 2.4 million followers on instagram.

j.drw

Jack creates really iteresting sketches by using lines and hatching to create shading. He really understands the planes of the face and acurately captures how people look. I aspire to be able to use pencil in this unique geometrical way.
